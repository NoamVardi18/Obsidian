#### קצת מתוך הספר
‏ ‏David Goggins was in the Navy SEALS. He had done Hell Week three times. It is the thing that makes you start your way as a SEAL so he's done it three times. He lost a lot of pounds but the shocking thing is that in his youth, when he was young, he was black and a lot of people just hated him for that. All his surround was just corrupted with people just not being good for him. His father was eating his mother and he saw his uncle die in front of his eyes and all of that stuff. It makes me think: how can one person be that good, like that physically good and motivated, when his past looked like that? It took something that was a depression and it turned it into something that was really special and that's what's special about him. I learned a lot of things just continuing to do the work when it's hard.
