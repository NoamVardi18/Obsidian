## Prompting System (learned this session)

### Perfect Prompt Structure

1. Who you are (role + credentials)
1. What you are going to do (task)
1. What the problem is
1. How to complete it (format, constraints)
1. Who I am (background, context)
1. Optional: tone, length, language
1. Validation: “Summarize what you understood before starting”

### AIM Framework

- **Actor**: define the expert with real credentials
- **Input**: rich context — data, constraints, goals
- **Mission**: specific complex output

### Key Learning Prompts

- Raw dump: “I just finished [book]. Here’s what I remember: [dump]. Sharpen this, fill what I missed, connect to my life.”
- Deep dive: “Actor: expert on [topic]. Input: [concept]. Mission: explain why this matters for someone in my situation.”
- Quiz: “Quiz me on [book].”
- Diffuse mode: “Write a 5-minute dialogue between two experts on [book]. Sharp, no filler. I’m going to listen to this.”
- Final note: “Go through everything we discussed about [book]. Build my final note — clean, structured, with my personal examples.”
